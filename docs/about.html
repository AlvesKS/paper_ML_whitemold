Summary

In the study we use machine learning interpretation for untangling effects of weather and soil variables on the white mold prevalence in snap bean filds of New York. White mold is a monocyclic plant disease caused by the Ascomycete Sclerotinia sclerotiorum, and it has the reputation of being one the most destructive disease around the world.
